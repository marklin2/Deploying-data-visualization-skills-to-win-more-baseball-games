Overview

Data Visualization has been applied to areas such as consulting and business practices, which is widely used in a great variety of domains. By following this step by step tutorial, readers are expected to acquire data visualization skills to extend the usage of large datasets. We will use modern baseball as the domain, and further showcase fancy graphs to discover invisible and findings.

We will start by exporting public datasets from Fangraphs, a website publishing Major League Baseball (MLB) sources. We will further clean and obtain key information via excel spreadsheets. Moreover, we will walk you through a user friendly but exhaustive data visualization software - Tableau. From downloading to creating your own graphs, readers are expected to spend an hour by carefully following the detailed instructions. Last but not least, by cultivating a data-driven culture, readers may demonstrate their knowledge by interacting with the datasets. To sum up, required materials such as discoveries, demonstrated techniques and references are listed on the Appendix page.




Evolution of Modern Analytics in Baseball

Big data is innately integrated in baseball, by taking a closer look on player's individual statistics and further zooming out to roster constructions and financial considerations, the usage of data has constantly evolved and never looked back. Professional baseball is about winning, which the implementation of anlaytics is to build a consistent winning team. With the help of mathematics, statistics and computer science, we are able to refine the meanings behind certain situations and figures to optimize player's performances on the fields.

Again, the discussion on percentage baseball and probability events does not guarantee specific outcomes, which is why baseball is always fun to watch!
